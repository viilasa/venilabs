Use this list before you announce your new site on Instagram or WhatsApp status. It catches issues that hurt **trust**, **conversions**, and **local search**.

## Contact and trust

- **Phone and WhatsApp** visible on every page (header or sticky button).
- **Physical address** or clear service area if you visit clients.
- **Hours** that match your Google Business Profile.
- **Email** on domain (e.g. hello@yourbusiness.com) if you use one.

## Mobile experience

- Tap targets large enough for thumbs.
- Forms short—ask only what you need for the first enquiry.
- No horizontal scrolling on common phones.

## Content basics

- **Unique title** and meta description per main page.
- **One primary topic** per page (do not merge five services into one wall of text).
- **Alt text** on important images (accessibility + context for search).

## Speed

- Images compressed; lazy-load below the fold where sensible.
- No blocking scripts that delay first paint.

## Legal and practical (India context)

- **Privacy note** if you collect forms or use analytics.
- **GST** or invoice mention if you quote prices professionally—when applicable.

## Post-launch

- Submit **sitemap** in Google Search Console.
- **Link** your site from your Google Business Profile.
- Ask a friend on a different network to **test** load speed and forms.

Solid **website design for Goa** small businesses is not only aesthetics—it is **clarity**, **speed**, and **consistency** with how you already show up offline and on social media.
