For most local businesses, over 80% of visitors come from mobile.

If the site is hard to read, slow to load, or unclear on what to do next, the lead is lost.

## Mobile-first conversion basics

1. One clear headline above the fold
2. Immediate call and WhatsApp buttons
3. Short service explanation
4. Social proof close to the CTA

Designing for mobile first means designing for real customer behavior.
